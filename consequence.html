Consequence

The collapse of the USSR has its good sides and bad sides. It left many unsolved problems but legacies as well existed. Some other countries or unions also got chances to expand themselves during this "feast".

American is the first beneficiary of this collapse. After the soviet union collapsed, American became the only dominant superpower on a global scale. Collapsed freed the U.S. government from any constraints imposed by a powerful rival. This allowed the U.S. government to intervene in other countries militarily without fear of any retaliation. Moreover, the EU(European Union)'s economy could prosper due to the end of the Cold War between the U.S. and USSR while the EU could extend its influence into areas that USSR once controlled.

The legacies inside the USSR were allocated as well. Since Russia inherited 3/4th of territory, population, resources, and the whole nuclear arsenal of its parent USSR, the membership of the UN(united union) would belong to Russia. The telecommunication code(+7) continues to be used by Russia and Kazakhstan while all Soviet embassies would replace by Russian embassies. Also, before its collapse, the soccer team of the soviet had just qualified for the 1992 UEFA European Football Championship. However, after the collapse, every republic in USSR would have to participate as an independent country, so FIFA allocating the Soviet team's record to Russia.

The collapse of the socialist U.S.S.R., as well as the other socialist countries of Europe, gave a serious punch to the ideology of communism among the world. Further, the acceptance of liberalization and liberal marketing economies by almost all the countries gave a further impact on the popularity of communism in the world.

Besides, the Russian economy continued to suffer from the remnant of the old structure. Although the soviet's command economy obtain great success before the 1950s, it declined after the 1980s. Gorbachev tries to recover the economy through his policy - Perestroika, but it only led to further economical chaos. This eventually made Russia has to economically relied on America after the collapse for a long period.
